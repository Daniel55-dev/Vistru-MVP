Recommended features for full MVP

Mobile PWA
Many clients and engineers are mobile-first. A PWA or React Native app is critical for CCTV feed access in the field.
💬
In-Platform Messaging
Secure threaded messaging with logs archived as evidence for any future arbitration.

SMS / WhatsApp Alerts
Push milestone alerts, payment confirmations, and AI reports via SMS/WhatsApp for clients with limited internet.
